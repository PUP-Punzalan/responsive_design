What is ISKODE LEGENDS?

Iskode is a team-based strategy game in which two teams of five formidable champions confront each other to annihilate the opposing base. Pick from a selection of over 140 champions to execute impressive maneuvers, secure eliminations, and bring down towers as you strive for triumph in the battlefield.
